Master Mobil UX video notes:

Desktop computer have been declining since 20011

Design Mobile First!

  • Responsive is NOT Mobile First
    • Doesn’t address screen real estate issues
    • Doesn’t address touch interaction issues

Downsides to Mobile First

  • Time consuming and costly to do a full rebuild

Upside to Mobile First

  • Provides best experience across many devices
  • Addresses constraints of mobile size/bandwidth --- What does this(bandwidth) mean?
  • Mobile first is planning for the future and growth

Start small and scale up

  • This insures you are focusing on you content and functionality
  • Going from small to big, things grow well
